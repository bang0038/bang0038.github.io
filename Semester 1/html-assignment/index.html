About Me

I am a Wedding Photographer here in Ottawa and a student in the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College. So far, I am really enjoying this program and am learning tons of new things. I joined this program because I wanted to strengthen my skills in technology and design and also apply what I learn to my own business.

I am a mom to a 1 year old baby girl. Between school, running my own business & being a mom life is very busy. In my free time I enjoy going to the gym, taking photos, hanging out with my friends, watching Netflix and playing video games, yes, I'm a cool mom. This is my 2nd time at AC and I am super excited to see where this program takes me.
